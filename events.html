    • EVENTS AT AESTHETIC

    Weddings

    Weddings at Restaurant Aesthetic offer an unforgettable blend of luxury and intimacy. The elegant decor, personalized menus, and exceptional service create a breathtaking atmosphere for your special day. Whether it’s a grand celebration or an intimate gathering.

    Wine Tasting

    Explore a world of fine wines with exclusive tastings at Restaurant Aesthetic. Guided by expert sommeliers, our curated selections offer a journey through premium vintages and regions. Paired with gourmet bites, each tasting elevates your understanding and appreciation of the art of wine.

    Jazz Nights

    Jazz Nights at Aesthetic combine the smooth sounds of live music with a chic, modern atmosphere. Sip on expertly crafted cocktails or fine wine as you relax in a stylish setting. With top-tier musicians performing regularly, every jazz night promises a soulful, memorable experience.

    Private Partys

    Host an unforgettable private party at Restaurant Aesthetic, where style and sophistication meet. With personalized menus, curated cocktails, and elegant decor, every detail is tailored to your event. Whether it’s a birthday, anniversary, or special occasion, Aesthetic ensures a memorable celebration.

    Curated Seasonal Menu

    Indulge in the essence of each season with our carefully curated menu. Crafted from the finest, locally-sourced ingredients, every dish reflects the deep, rich flavors of the moment. Immerse yourself in a culinary journey where every plate tells a story of freshness and finesse.

    Exclusive Chef’s Table

    For a truly immersive experience, the Chef’s Table invites you to dine behind the scenes. In the intimate glow of the kitchen, witness the art of fine dining unfold before you. Each course, custom-designed to your taste, elevates the evening into an unforgettable, bespoke experience.

    Sustainably Excellence

    At Aesthetic, luxury meets responsibility. Our commitment to sustainability runs deep, from organic produce to ethically sourced ingredients. Each dish reflects our dedication to preserving the earth while delivering unparalleled quality, ensuring that indulgence never comes at a cost to the environment.
