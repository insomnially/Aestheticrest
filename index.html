    MENU

    Experience a culinary journey where texture, flavor, and harmony shine. Aesthetic presents a refined six- or eight-course tasting menu for lunch and dinner, with a special ‘Aesthetic to Lunch’ four-course option available on weekends. Each dish highlights rare ingredients, carefully crafted for perfect balance and taste.

    Indulge in a dining journey where every bite is a harmony of texture and taste. At Aesthetic, explore a carefully crafted menu featuring five or seven courses, tailored to match the evening’s ambiance. On weekdays, experience the “Aesthetic Brunch,” offering three refined courses. Each dish presents seasonal ingredients, highlighting creativity and precision in every detail.

    Chef Andrei Shakov

    Renowned as one of the finest chefs in the world, Andrei Shakov redefines the art of cooking with his innovative techniques and relentless pursuit of perfection. His creations blend traditional craftsmanship with modern influences, creating dishes that are as visually stunning as they are flavorful.

    Chef Manny Barella

    Manny Barella is a rising culinary talent, currently serving as the Culinary Director at Aesthetic. Originally from Monterrey, Mexico, Manny shifted from law school to pursue his passion for cooking, which led him to prestigious kitchens like the Michelin-starred Solage Resort and the five-star Frasca Food & Wine.

    Wine Collection

    The wine collection at Aesthetic restaurant is a carefully curated selection that mirrors the establishment’s philosophy of balancing sophistication with minimalism. It features both renowned global wines and rare bottles from boutique vineyards, with a focus on eco-friendly production and unique terroirs. The collection highlights French and Italian wines, including Bordeaux and Tuscan reserves, while also offering selections from New Zealand, Argentina, and Australia. Special emphasis is placed on natural, organic, and biodynamic wines, aligning with trends toward clean flavors and minimal intervention in winemaking. The restaurant’s sommelier is ready to provide recommendations for each course, from crisp white wines that pair perfectly with light appetizers to rich reds for the main dishes. Champagne holds a prominent place in the collection, with options for both celebratory occasions and a casual evening with friends. At Aesthetic, the wine list is not overwhelming but offers something unique for every guest, from connoisseurs of aged classics to those who enjoy exploring bold and experimental flavors.
